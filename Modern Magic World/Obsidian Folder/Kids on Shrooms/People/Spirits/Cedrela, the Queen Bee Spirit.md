How old is your character?

What is your appearance like? 
In spirit form, she is a bee about the size of a squirrel. She has a yellow and black lacing of her skin that runs down her body, giving the appearance of almost a dress. 

In her beauty (human form), she is an early 30s white feminine woman. She has her hair tied back into a shoulder length black pony tail. She has a sundress that goes to her knees that is yellow and black. She wears sneakers and black gloves.
