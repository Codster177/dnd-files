## Character Breakdown:
How old is your character? 
Weylin is 25 years old. His birthday is on 1/7/1596, or the Seventh of First Spring, 1596.

What is your appearance like? 
Weylin has white masculine features. He is 5'9" with short dark brown hair and lots of piercings down his ears. He is quite fit and slender.

What is your personality like?
Weylin is passionate about teaching and once he gets into his groove his is confident. Outside of that, he is usually pretty nervous in all other aspects of life.

What are your character's fears?
Weylin fears not getting taken seriously by the students or the other teachers. He is kind and wants the students to like him, but is afraid of being a pushover.
