
We are calling Adversity Tokens "Stupid Points". They can be used to boost a die roll to meet a DC or explode the die once the respective value hits the max die number.

## Starting Strengths:

| Strength:           | Description:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Attractive          | When performing a check to persuade and increase opinions others have of you, each AT is worth +2 instead of +1.                                                                                                                                                                        |
| Cool Under Pressure | You can spend 2 AT to remain calm during an intense and stressful situation. (GM's Discretion)                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| Cultured            | You can spend 2 AT to relate to a group that follows a specific culture well enough to convince them to welcome you and your friends.                                                                                                                                                   |
| Easygoing           | Gain 2 Adversity Tokens when you fail a check instead of one.                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| Gifted              | If you make a check involving the Magic Die that fails, you may spend 1 AT to reroll the Magic Die. You cannot do this multiple times per roll.                                                                                                                                         |
| Gross               | You have some kind of gross trick or spell (loud, quiet, smelly, up to you) that you can do on command.                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| Guardian            | When defending another player or an NPC, always roll both your stat die and the magic die, then add them together, even if you're not using magic to do it. If your check fails, you may reroll the magic die.                                                                          |
| Handy               | You gain the benefits of user your spellcasting focus without having to have it active.                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| Heroic              | If your character is afraid and in face of danger, your character can spend 2 AT to have advantage on a roll to face the danger head on.                                                                                                                                                |
| Innocence           | Once per session, you may spend 2 AT to convince an adult to not get you in trouble for a minor transgression. (GM's Discretion)                                                                                                                                                        |
| Intuitive           | You can spend 2 AT to ask the GM about your surroundings, an NPC, or the like. (Basically, make a free successful insight check) The GM must answer honestly. (GM's Discretion)                                                                                                         |
| Loyal               | Each of the AT you spend to help your friends gives them +2 instead of a +1.                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| Lucky               | You may spend 2 AT to reroll a stat check. It does not allow you to reroll the Magic Die.                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| Perfectionist       | Anytime a plan goes exactly as intended, gain 2 AT. If a stat levels up during the remainder of the session and you still have these 2 AT, you can spend them to level that stat up twice.                                                                                              |
| Prepared            | Spend 2 AT to just happen to have one commonplace item with you. (GM's Discretion)                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| Psychic Link        | You can spend 1 AT to form a psychic link with one person that you can see and that has a close bond with you. This link lasts until you end it and allows you to freely communicate with each other. While this link is active, you can spend 1 AT to see through the recipients eyes. |
| Resilient           | When spending AT to boost a check that is defending yourself, each AT is worth +2 instead of +1.                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| Resourceful         | Spend 1 AT to find a mundane but useful item in your surroundings.                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| Spell Slinger       | You have advantage on spell attacks that your character is making emotionally and recklessly. However, any roll that may be involved in trying to keep these attacks from affecting bystanders has disadvantage.                                                                        |
| Tough               | Once per session, you can spend 3 AT to brush off an attack towards your character that would otherwise require a roll, completely negating any negative effects. (GM's Discretion)                                                                                                     |
| Unassuming          | Once per session, you can spend 2 AT to not be seen. (GM's Discretion)                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| Wild                | You can spend 1 AT to communicate with an animal.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
## Special Strengths:

| Strength:    | Description:                                                                                  |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Spirit       | You can communicate with any animal, and you can spend 2 AT to communicate with plants.       |
| Witch's Aura | You have an effect that is automatically effecting people who enter a certain radius of you.  |
