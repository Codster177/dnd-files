## Overview
The Age of Discovery was the Era in which Wizards are first born into this world through the discovery of wizardry and the founding of [[The Grand Aviran Empire]]. This Era showcases the development of wizardry as an impressive and ever developing art of spellcasting, that eventually gets driven to limits that intrude on the rest of human civilization, and then onto the natural world.

During this Era, [[The Grand Aviran Empire]] grows into a small but strong empire under the rule of [[Abeus Algori]].
